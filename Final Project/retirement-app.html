How does a 140 year old company transition into the digital age and redefine the financial wellness experience? The Retirement App provided easy access to retirement capabilties and personalized financial guidance. Through a bold focus on our users we were able to drive behavior change in our customerâ€™s retirement journey.

Phase 1

Quick Win

What was the problem?

How do we keep our place in the app store ratings? Our current app has 1 star and is at risk of being kicked off the app store.

how did we solve it?

We needed a bandaid solution to stop the bleeding. The team spent two weeks refreshing the interface to a modern and clean design. We focused strictly on the colors and the overall interface design.

Quick Redesign

Using our brand colors we redesigned the outdated interface to make it feel modern and apporachable.

Phase 2

Feature Set

What was the problem?

How do we create a product that provides greater value to our customers and enables them to have a centralized retirement and financial wellness literacy?

how did we solve it?

We conducted several workshops, design sprints with all members from the project (business partners, design team and tech partners) to create a product that drove customer behavior change

Feature 1

Retirement Contributions

One of the core functionalities in Retirement. Making contributing to retirement easier for our customers, and encourage them to reach their company match.

Feature 2

Transfers & Allocations

Easily allow our clients to transfer funds between their accounts and understand how their money moves.

Feature 3

Goalmaker

Allows users to enroll in a featuer that creates a customized retirement plan based on their selected retirement age. They'd also have the option to customize further.

Process

Behind the Scenes

What was our process?

How do we create a product that provides greater value to our customers and enables them to have a centralized retirement and financial wellness literacy?

Research

Using surveys, meeting with business partners and stakeholders, and Usertesing we got a better understanding our customers' needs. Afterwards we created user two personas that conveyed the most probable mindset in our users.

Brainstorming & Workshops

The group got together to run a 5 day design sprint where we made a functional prototype that we tested with our users.

Userflows & Wireframes

As a group we created a user flow that showed the journey of clients enrolling, registering, contributing, and transferring funds in their retirement account.
